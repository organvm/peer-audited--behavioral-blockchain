# Telos: The Idealized Form of Styx

## The Dream
Styx is conceived as a decentralized, peer-audited behavioral market that transforms relationship recovery from a private struggle into a verifiable, game-theoretic process. It weaponizes loss aversion (coefficient 1.955) to enforce the "No Contact" rule, utilizing financial stakes to create a "Blockchain of Truth."

## Theoretical Grounding
- **Behavioral Economics**: Leveraging prospect theory and loss aversion to create high-stakes commitment devices.
- **The Fury Network**: A decentralized anti-cheat system where peer auditors are incentivized to expose fraudulent claims.
- **Digital Exhaust**: Server-side verification of communication artifacts to prove/disprove behavioral compliance.

## The Goal
To prove that human behavioral commitment can be engineered, audited, and verified through a combination of financial incentives and decentralized social consensus.
