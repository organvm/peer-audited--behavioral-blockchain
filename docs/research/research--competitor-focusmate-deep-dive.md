## 1. Executive Summary
Focusmate is the leading platform for virtual "Body Doubling"—a technique where two people co-work on camera to increase focus. It is highly effective for neurodivergent (ADHD) users who need a "Social Anchor" to initiate tasks. However, its reliance on **Passive Social Pressure** and its lack of **Performance Penalties** create a significant integrity gap. Users can easily "fake" focus while on camera, and the platform has no mechanism to enforce task completion. Styx parries this by adding **Financial Stakes** and **Artifact-Based Auditing** to the social co-working model.

## 3. Gap Identification (The "Shatter Point")

### Shatter Point 1: Passive vs. Active Accountability
*   **The Bug:** Focusmate is a "Presence" tracker, not a "Productivity" tracker. A user can keep their camera on while browsing distractions on another screen or playing with their phone off-camera. The partner rarely calls out this behavior to avoid social awkwardness.
*   **The Opportunity:** Styx requires **Artifacts of Action** (Digital Exhaust). We don't just care that you were at your desk; we verify that the code was committed, the document was saved, or the messages were *not* sent (No Contact).

### Shatter Point 2: The "No-Show" Friction
*   **The Bug:** Despite ratings, "flaking" is common. If a partner no-shows, the other user's motivation often collapses. There is no financial penalty for wasting a partner's time.
*   **The Opportunity:** Styx uses **Staked Appointments**. If you book a "Fury Session" or a recovery check-in and no-show, your stake is slashed and paid to the inconvenienced partner or the audit pool.

### Shatter Point 3: High Synchronous Friction
*   **The Bug:** Focusmate requires a 50-minute commitment at a specific time. This is incompatible with many high-volatility scenarios where a user needs immediate, asynchronous accountability.
*   **The Opportunity:** Styx's **Fury Network** works asynchronously. You provide proof when you can, and the audit happens in the background. This allows for 24/7 enforcement without needing to find a matching partner in real-time.
