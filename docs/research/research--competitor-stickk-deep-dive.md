## 4. Gap Identification (The "Shatter Point")

### The Oracle Problem: "Friendly Collusion"
The core weakness of stickK is the **Referee System**.
*   **The Bug:** Committers invite their friends as referees. Friends are socially incentivized *not* to trigger a financial penalty.
*   **The Hack:** Users engage in "Referee Swaps" on forums (e.g., Reddit) where strangers agree to rubber-stamp each other's reports regardless of actual behavior.
*   **The Friction:** If a referee is unresponsive, the contract stalls or fails, leading to user frustration.
