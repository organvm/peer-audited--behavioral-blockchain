# Audience Growth Engine — Styx/Jessica Live Instance

This is the worked instantiation of `docs/playbooks/playbook--audience-growth-engine.md` for
Styx's Phase-1 wedge, with Jessica as Host. It is the proof that the engine runs. It **cross-
links** the canonical Styx detail rather than restating it:

- Channel split, ladder, ratios, cadence, empty-room rules, first-cohort model →
  `planning--dual-channel-audience-architecture--2026-03-10.md`
- 5-level attack, audience hierarchy, "what not to do yet", positioning →
  `planning--market-attack-plan--2026-03-10.md`
- Governed growth organism (artifacts, CRIT/HEAL, health bands) → `docs/departments/gro/REGE.md`

## Why this exists

Jessica has grown her following ~18k → ~20k and is paying the technical partner $100/hr to
keep building it. The opportunity is to make that spend do double duty: grow Jessica's
**standalone audience asset** *and* feed Styx's no-contact-recovery beta. This instance turns
that intent into an executable system and produces the concrete assets to run it (calendar,
asset pack, outreach, metrics, engagement log).

## Goal hierarchy

1. **Grow Jessica's audience as an asset** — 20k → 25k engaged followers + a real owned email
   list (this is valuable even if zero people ever join Styx).
2. **Convert a qualified slice into the Styx funnel** — waitlist → first cohort (15–30).
3. **Build a repeatable system** — every asset here is a filled template that the next venture
   re-runs.

---

## A. The Five Parameters (Playbook §1)

- **P1 Host:** Jessica — **personal creator** archetype. Highest trust + emotional range; she
  owns a narrative arc inside the niche, not just "promotes the app."
- **P2 Wedge:** No-contact breakup recovery (the live urge moment: late-night texting, ex
  social-media checking, emotional backsliding).
- **P3 Product:** Styx iOS private beta (test-money pilot, US allowlist) — *and*, per the
  audience-as-product model, Jessica's following itself.
- **P4 Owned Asset:** Waitlist + email list + qualified beta roster + referral list.
- **P5 Proof Loop:** No-contact milestone cards (7 / 14 / 30 days) — shareable proof that
  pulls the next struggler in.

## B. The Ladder (Playbook §2 → dual-channel doc "First Cohort Model")

- **Admission rule:** fit with no-contact wedge · likelihood of actual use · willingness to
  give feedback.
- **First cohort size:** 15–30.
- **Weekly ritual:** founder check-in + weekly prompt + one milestone highlight + selective
  referral invite.
- **Source mix:** Jessica audience · creator referrals · practitioner referrals.

## C. Dual-Channel Setup (Playbook §3 → dual-channel doc)

- **Jessica (Host) channel:** names the pain, captures the urge moment, human voice; does NOT
  become a brochure or lead with "behavioral blockchain"/financial framing.
- **Styx (Product) channel:** product receipts, beta scope, trust/safety, early-user proof;
  does NOT try to be the intimate primary voice.
- **Ratios (from dual-channel doc):** Jessica 60% niche value / 25% personal-founder / 15%
  Styx conversion · Styx 40% product clarity / 25% trust-scope / 20% proof / 15% conversion.
- **Fixed CTA set:**
  - Jessica: `Join the Private Beta` · `Get the no-contact guide` · `Try the Do Not Text Your Ex Tonight tool`
  - Styx: `Apply for Beta` · `See how it works` · `Read the beta scope`

## D. Five-Level Attack (Playbook §4 → market-attack-plan)

- **L1 Demand Capture:** high-intent breakup/no-contact search ("how to survive 30 days of no
  contact", "why willpower fails after a breakup", "stop texting my ex"). Capture page + the
  "Do Not Text Your Ex Tonight" emergency tool. *(See market-attack-plan Level 1.)*
- **L2 Borrowed Audience:** breakup-recovery / attachment-style / sober-curious / therapy
  creators on TikTok, IG, YouTube, Substack, podcasts. Outreach via
  `planning--jessica-content-asset-pack--2026-06-01.md` §4. *(market-attack-plan Level 2.)*
- **L3 Intermediary Distribution:** therapists & breakup coaches as accountability
  infrastructure for their clients (ties to the B2B ICP at `docs/departments/b2b/artifacts/icp.md`).
- **L4 Community Loop:** milestone share cards (P5) + curated cohort invites + controlled
  referrals.
- **L5 Authority Layer:** founder essays on why willpower fails after a breakup, the
  accountability gap, and consequence-backed recovery — Jessica + Styx co-voiced.
- **Do-not-say-yet (niche-specific):** no "real-money winnings", no Fury/blockchain lead, no
  paid acquisition before organic baseline, no broad community before cohort motion, no
  guarantees about the relationship or the user's emotional outcome.

rung as the flagship CTA, never replacing the free value.

Inherits `docs/departments/gro/REGE.md` CRIT:content-quality + human checkpoints. Niche

never exploit relapse for engagement-bait, never promise relationship/health outcomes
(Recovery/Aegis spirit).

## 30 / 60 / 90 Roadmap

- **Days 0–30 — Ignition:** stand up both channels' content engine; publish Jessica week-1
  batch; ship the no-contact lead magnet + emergency tool capture page; open the waitlist;
  begin L2 creator outreach (first 20 targets). Target: +1–2k followers, first 300 email
  opt-ins, 50+ qualified waitlist.
- **Days 31–60 — First Room:** admit Cohort 1 (15–30); start the weekly ritual; begin L3
  intermediary outreach; first milestone share cards (P5) go live as proof. Target: Cohort 1
  active, first 7-day milestone proofs.
- **Days 61–90 — Compounding:** Authority-layer essays (L5); controlled referrals; first
  low-ticket audience-product test; evaluate channel performance and reallocate. Target:
  25k followers trajectory, Cohort 1 → 30-day completions, repeatable proof loop.
