## Health bands (aligned to GRO REGE §9 where applicable)

- **Green:** weekly content shipped on schedule (0 missed in trailing 4) · follower + email
  growth on target · waitlist filling.
- **Yellow:** 1 missed content week in trailing 4 · growth flat/below target · waitlist stalling.
- **Red:** 2+ missed content weeks · net follower/email decline · zero qualified waitlist for
  2+ weeks. (Trigger a channel-efficiency review per REGE CRIT:channel-efficiency.)

## Review cadence

Weekly review tied to the $100/hr engagement (`planning--audience-growth-engagement-economics--2026-06-01.md`):
cost = hours × $100 vs return = net followers + net email + net qualified waitlist. Reallocate
effort off any channel that's out of line (REGE HEAL:channel-death spirit).
