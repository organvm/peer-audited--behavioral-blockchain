## 3-Touch Warm Sequence (creators)

### Touch 1 — Genuine engagement (Day 0, public)
Before any DM: meaningfully engage with 2–3 of their recent posts. No ask. Build recognition.

### Touch 2 — The DM (Day 2)
> Hi {first_name} — [specific, true compliment about a recent piece]. I work on {one-line
> on the venture in their audience's language, not internal jargon}. I'm not pitching — I think
> our audiences overlap and I have an idea that helps *your* people. Open to hearing it?

### Touch 3 — The offer (on reply)
Offer value first. Pick what fits:
- early/private access for them and their audience
- a free {lead magnet / tool} they can share
- a collaboration (guest content, co-created resource, takeover)
- advisor / founding-partner status for select fits

## Intermediary Sequence (practitioners / operators)

### Touch 1 — Intro (Day 1)
> Hi {first_name}, I came across {practice/org} via {source} and noticed you work with
> {audience}. We built {venture} as {infrastructure framing — what it does for *their*
> clients/users}. Would a 15-minute walkthrough be useful?

### Touch 2 — Proof (Day 4)
Share one concrete result or a one-pager. No hard sell.

### Touch 3 — Soft close (Day 9)
> Totally fine if the timing's off — want me to send the one-pager so it's there when it's
> relevant?

## Guardrails

- No false familiarity, no fake urgency, no mass-blast copy-paste.
- Respect each platform's DM/outreach norms.
- Consent required before naming any partner publicly (testimonial rule).
