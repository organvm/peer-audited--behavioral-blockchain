# Template: Content Asset Pack

The concrete, copy-paste layer. Fill brackets with niche language. Everything here must pass
the host organism's content-quality guardrails (no medical/financial claims, no jargon lead,
empathy-first in sensitive niches).

## 1. Hook Bank (write 15–25)

Hooks are the first line. Group by angle.

- **Pain-naming:** "[name the exact feeling the audience has but can't articulate]"
- **Pattern-interrupt:** "[the thing everyone does that quietly makes it worse]"
- **Permission/reframe:** "[you're not [negative self-label]. you're [human truth].]"
- **Contrarian:** "[the advice everyone gives that's wrong for this niche]"
- **Story-open:** "[in-medias-res moment that lands the audience inside a scene]"

## 2. Post Skeletons (reusable structures)

- **The list:** hook → 3–5 punchy items → 1-line turn → soft CTA.
- **The micro-story:** scene → tension → small realization → universal takeaway.
- **The reframe:** common belief → why it fails here → better frame → CTA.
- **The receipt:** [product proof / screenshot / result] → what it means → CTA.

## 3. Story-Sequence Frames (multi-card)

- **Card 1:** hook / pain. **Card 2:** the moment. **Card 3:** the turn.
  **Card 4:** the tool/offer. **Card 5:** CTA + how to act now.
